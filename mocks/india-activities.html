Ideas to Explore

🎓

India Study Abroad Programs

Summer language immersion programs in India (Tamil Nadu, Delhi, etc.) offered through US universities or nonprofit orgs.

🧘

Yoga / Classical Music Certification

Structured certification in carnatic music or yoga can show depth of cultural engagement beyond surface participation.

📚

Indian-American Youth Organizations

Organizations like BAPS Swaminarayan, Hindu Students Council (college chapter), or TiE Young Entrepreneurs.

🌿

NGO Partnerships for Service

Akshaya Patra, Teach for India, or Isha Foundation all have structured teen volunteer opportunities in India.
